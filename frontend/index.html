Dispute Prevention Navigator

Do you know what "appointments" are?

An appointment is when someone is officially given a role in a building project, such as Client, Principal Designer, or Principal Contractor.

Do you know what the client's responsibilites are?

Give information, choose competent people, keep safety documents, and make sure regulations are followed.

Do you know what the designer's responsiblities are?

Follow Parts B, F, L, O, consider safety risks, and record decisions.

Do you know what Regulation 38 is?

It requires giving fire-safety information (strategy, alarms, escape routes) to the building owner at completion

Do you know what Building Safety Act 2022 is??

A law that improves building safety by requiring risk management and accurate safety records.

Do you know what the responsibilities for Part B (Fire Safety) are?

Provide safe escape routes, alarms, fire doors, smoke control, and fire-safety info.

Do you know what the responsibilities for Part F (Ventilation) are?

Provide good airflow, fresh air, ventilation systems if needed, and testing info.

Do you know what the responsibilities for Part L (Energy Efficiency) are?

Use insulation, low‚Äëcarbon heating, airtightness tests, SAP calculations, and provide EPC.

Do you know what the responsibilities for Part O (Overheating) are?

Use shading, ventilation, and limit solar gain to keep indoor temps safe.

Do you know what documents we're supposed to keep?

TDesigns, calculations, test results, certificates, maintenance info, and fire‚Äësafety handover pack.

Duty holder Responsibilities

The Building Safety Act 2022 places legal duties on Clients, Designers, Principal Designers and Principal Contractors.

  • Identify safety risks early
  • Appoint competent duty holders
  • Keep clear design and safety records
  • Ensure work complies with Building Regulations

Regulation 38

Fire-safety information must be provided to the building owner at completion.

  • Fire strategy documents
  • Fire doors and fire-stopping details
  • Escape routes and alarm systems
  • Maintenance instructions

Handover Guidance

At completion, the building must be handed over safely with clear information.

  • Provide Regulation 38 fire-safety pack
  • Provide ventilation and energy test results
  • Issue updated drawings and manuals
  • Ensure information is clear and accessible
